The Concepts of Time Complexity and Space Complexity

Time Complexity is how much an algorithm is efficient in terms of time taken by that algorithm for the complete execution of the program to get a desired result.

Space Complexity is how much space or memory is required by the algorithm to complete its execution to get a desired output. The space complexity is measured in terms of O(n)

Space Complexity should be optimum so that the program do not get stacke over flow exception.
